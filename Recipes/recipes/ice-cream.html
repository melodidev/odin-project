Oatmeal

Description

It is for summer!

And maybe for winter, just in case you are living in Southern Hemisphere.

Ingredients

  • 500 ml milk
  • Two tablespoons of starch
  • 200 gr cream
  • Four tablespoons of sugar
  • One tablespoon of vanilla

Steps

  1. Mix all the ingredients.
  2. Stir and cook on medium heat until it reaches pudding consistency.
  3. If you want it with cocoa, add two dessert spoons of cocoa after it cools down.
  4. Put it in the freezer, mix it a few times in a couple of hours so that it does not freeze.
